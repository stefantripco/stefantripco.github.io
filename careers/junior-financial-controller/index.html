Junior Financial Controller

Junior Financial Controller

Tripco is growing! We are seeking a talented and motivated Junior Financial Controller to join our Finance team in our Cape Town office, reporting directly to our Financial Manager. This is an exciting opportunity for a positive, proactive self-starter who is eager to assist and take full ownership of their responsibilities.


SOME OF THE THINGS YOU WILL BE RESPONSIBLE FOR:

Accounting:

  • Classification of accounting transactions and review of relevant supporting documents
  • Monthly bank reconciliations
  • Monthly invoicing and issuing of purchase orders
  • Accounting reconciliation up until the trial balance
  • Prepare monthly management account

Audit

  • Ensure and observe segregation of duties
  • Prepare working papers for the annual audit
  • Liaise with and assist auditors

Taxation

  • Completion of all tax returns on E-filing for review
  • Income tax, dividend tax, provisional tax, VAT201s, EMP201s

Human resources support (HR)

  • Assist with all administrative duties of payroll
  • Monthly payroll compilation

Company payments

  • Creating and maintaining good relationships with all suppliers
  • Reconciling supplier billing statements with detailed reports
  • Loading of supplier payments


WHAT YOU'LL NEED:

  • Accuracy
  • Meticulous attention to detail
  • Ability to organise and prioritise
  • Ability to perform under pressure
  • Be deadline-driven
  • Able to work independently and in a team environment
  • Adaptable and able to learn and develop within the role
  • Solution-orientated and proactive


BACKGROUND:

  • Degree in Bcom Finance or any Accounting related qualifications
  • Completed SAIPA / CIMA articles or 3 years of working experience in an accounting or finance department
  • Good understanding of payroll and employee tax
  • Experience in Quickbooks Online & Payspace would be advantageous
  • Thorough understanding of finance function and sub-processes


BENEFITS:

  • Hybrid working arrangement (part-time remote)
  • Retirement fund benefits
  • Monthly half-day leave (6 additional leave days per annum)
  • Staff discount on our travel platforms
  • Company laptop and accessories
  • Additional employee perks such as additional leave days for long service, monthly staff events with the entire team, and more
